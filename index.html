Projects

Philadelphia's Digital Divide Analysis

R

Analyzed the relationship between household income and internet access across Philadelphia census tracts using R. Created choropleth map visualizing percentage of households without internet access, bar chart showing broadband access by income brackets, and scatter plot comparing linear vs logistic regression models. Used US Census Bureau ACS 2022 data to examine digital equity patterns across the city.

Indego Bike Share Analysis

ArcGIS Pro

Spatial analysis of Philadelphia's Indego bike share system examining station distribution, usage patterns, and accessibility across neighborhoods. Evaluated service equity and identified potential expansion areas to improve sustainable transportation access.
